About my favourite food; Afang soup

Brief history

Afang soup

Afang soup; a delight to behold and to eat. This delicious soup is native to the Efiks, the major occupant of (Cross River & Akwa Ibom State). Afang soup is made from Afang leaves (otherwise known as wild spinach, Okazi, Koko, fumbua or eru) and Waterleaf. Afang Soup is soup is a soup rich in meats and vegetables and palm oil and served alongside Nigerian swallow such as eba, pounded yam and semovita. The originators of this dish love to add a lot of seafood to afang but I love sticking to the few in this soup which are stockfish and dry fish, periwinkles with the shells and of course snails.

Prep Time:15 mins
Cook Time:35 mins
Total Time:50 mins
Course: Soup
Cuisine: African
Keyword: one pot
Servings: 4

Ingredients (for 6 servings)

Basic Ingredients
Ingredients Quantity
Okazi or Afang leaf, grounded 5 cups
Spinach or Water leaf, chopped 1 lb (680g)
Palm oil 1/4 cup
Meat (goat meat &tripe) 2 lb
Crayfish 1 cup
Salt I teaspoon
scotch bonnets 4
Dry or smoked fish, deboned desired quantity
Onion 1 medium sized
Stock cubes 3
Stock fish desired size

Steps

PREPARE MEAT

  • Wash goat meat and tripe (or meat of choice) and place it into a pot
  • In a blender add 1 onion and 2 scotch bonnets. Blend into a paste and pour into the pot of meat
  • Season the meat with salt, cameroon pepper, paprika and 2 stock cubes. Place on medium heat and boil for 30 minutes or till soft.
  • Soak dry fish in hot water for 5 minutes to soften it

PREP THE AFANG LEAVES

  • While meat is boiling, wash and soak your sliced okazi leaves in hot water for 2-3 minutes to soften it
  • Once soft, transfer the leaves to a blender and blend with a small amount of water to your desired consistency.
  • Wash and chopped your spinach

PREPARE SOUP

  • In the pot of meat add dry fish, palm oil , crayfish, stock cubes and salt. Stir well
  • Add the blended okazi and spinach or waterleaf and combine well. Let it cook for 8 minutes or until the vegetables begins to wilt. Then garnish with 1 scotch bonnet
  • taste and season to taste.Turn off the heat and let it simmer till it cools down
  • garnish with scotch bonnet serve and ENJOY!
